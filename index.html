Precautions
  • Do not use this product for people under 12 years old, people with coagulation disorders, or people with skin allergies.
  • Do not use this product at altitudes above 2000 meters, as it may cause danger.
  • Use with caution for people with loose skin, ischemia, or blood circulation disorders.
  • Clean and dry the skin surface before use, otherwise the blood sample may be affected.
  • If bleeding continues at the collection site, use appropriate methods to stop the bleeding and seek medical attention promptly.

Instructions for Capillary Blood Collection Device

Step 1: Preparation
Open the packaging, check the contents of the box
Before operation, please wash hands or wear gloves
Step 2: Warm Up
Activate warming pack
Warm arm for 5 minutes, if the arm becomes uncomfortable, stop warming for a while and continue
5-Minute Warming Timer
05:00
Time's up!
Step 3: Disinfection
Clean with alcohol wipe and allow to dry
Step 4: Blood Collection
Peel off the paper liners on the top and bottom, stick the device to upper arm
Stick to upper arm, please ensure the slot is positioned at the bottom
Insert the blood collection container vertically into the slot. The needle puncturing the rubber stopper signals the start of blood collection. Set the timer for 90 seconds.
90-Second Blood Collection Timer
01:30
Time's up!
Step 5: Stop Collection
Remove device if blood fills to the line before time is up
Remove device
Step 6: Remove Tube
Hold the container upright and remove container
Immediately shake the blood sample with the tube rubber stopper facing downward
Step 7: Post-processing
Clean with gauze. Apply a bandage
Follow local guidelines for discarding all materials
Pack sample and ship to the lab
